The Tallest...

Published by Dr Watts 29/060/2017

There are many creatures in the world that holds their identity in one or other unique way. World is full of amazing and beautiful living and non-living things that are known for their art, craft, design, cost, etc. In this post, we will discuss about the 10 tallest things in the world that not only holds the record of being tall but also surprises us with the masterminds behind their making. Listed below are the 10 tallest things in the world with their details and information.

Building- Burj Khalifa

Burj Khalifa besides being a tallest building in the world also holds many other records. The construction of this Dubai based skyscraper began in year 2004 and completed in 2010. This tallest building in the world is situated on level 124 of the World’s Tallest Tower, Burj Khalifa.

Man- Robert Pershing Wadlow

Robert Pershing Wadlow (February 22, 1918 – July 15, 1940), also known as the Alton Giant and the Giant of Illinois, was an American who became famous as the tallest person ever in human recorded history for whom there is irrefutable evidence.[3] The Alton and Illinois monikers reflect the fact that he was born and raised in Alton, Illinois.[1] Wadlow reached 8 ft 11.1 in (2.72 m)[2][4][5] in height and weighed 439 lb (199 kg) at his death at age 22. His great size and his continued growth in adulthood were due to hyperplasia of his pituitary gland, which results in an abnormally high level of human growth hormone. He showed no indication of an end to his growth even at the time of his death.

Tree- Redwood trees

The Redwood Trees are the tallest living beings in the earth. With the height of 360 feet (100+ meters) The world’s tallest trees are California redwoods, technically known as Sequoia sempervirens. Located in the South Grove, the largest redwood in Calaveras Big Trees State Park is the Louis Agassiz tree.

Statue- The Spring Temple Buddha

Located in the Zhaocun Township of Lushan County, Henan, China, The Spring Temple Buddha is the largest statue in the world with height of 153 meters (502 ft). The statue was completed in 2002.

Woman- Zeng Jinlian

Born in June 25, 1964, Zeng Jinlian is a tallest woman in the world with the height of 8 feet, 1.75 inches. She is also the only female counted among the 16 individuals in medical history who reached a verified eight feet or more.
